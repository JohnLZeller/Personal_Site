Volunteering

English Conversation Tutor

I volunteered at Portland Community College in the Student Resources Center as an English Conversation Partner. Once or twice a week I would meet with ESOL (English Speakers of Other Languages) students to speak with them and help them to improve their English language skills.

Post-Katrina Cleanup in New Orleans, LA

In Deccember of 2009, I traveled to New Orleans and volunteered for several days in the community. Among the work we did while there was stripping a community members home of lead paint, priming it with non-lead based paint, stripping/painting benches and cleaning up debris in a park near lake Pontchartrain.

Village Building Convergence 2010

In May of 2010, I volunteered as a building at the Village Building Convergence being held at PCC. Each year thre is a Village Building Convergence with tens of locations around Portland, Oregon. This particular one was building a cob structure in the Organic garden at PCC Sylvania campus.

Flood Cleanup in Vernonia, OR

In October of 2009, I volunteered to help clean up a river bank in Vernonia, Oregon. Vernonia had been the location of massive flooding earlier in the year which dislocated many families and put the entire town in financial strain.

Martin Luther King Jr. Day Volunteering

In January of 2010, I volunteered for Martin Luther King Jr. Day by walking the streets to clean up trash and deliver it to the proper municipal waste management facilities in Portland, Oregon.
